>_ welcome to my website!

Hello all, I’m Mercutio! Also known online as Sanster, Andromeda, or Madeline. This website is a place where I can write or post about myself, things that I like, or things happening in my life. My “little corner of the Internet”, as people say.

I know I could still do this on traditional social media, but they’ve all felt very same-ey to me lately, and I find the lack of customization quite sad. The amount of stupid AI features being forced onto me also helped me make up my mind and step away from them for good. I like the idea of having a page that reflects my personality and likes, without having to constantly worry about it looking professional or appealing.

So I decided, might as well make good use of my unemployment license (CS Degree) to make this small wish of mine a reality. Feel free to look around, and thank you for stopping by!

>_ resources

~ Sharing is caring! ~

Because good things are even better when shared. Here is a list of resources I used to create this website.

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
    • Pretty self explanatory.
  • JQuery
    • Streamlines a lot of JavaScript event handling. I find it faster and cleaner than vanilla JS.
  • W3Schools
    • Tons of useful tutorials for learning or refreshing your knowledge of HTML/CSS/JS.
  • 98.css
    • CSS library to easily recreate windows-98-styled windows!
  • CSS-Tricks
    • "A website about making websites."
    • This is where I got the Typewriter animation and the old-timey terminal window styling.
  • LoveBerry
    • A modified version of this was used for the Music Player. This website also has other useful code snippets and is super-cool overall!!
  • W98 Icons
    • PNG collection of all windows 98 icons in different sizes!
    • Used for the little icons on the side sections.
  • Aleclownes
    • Used for the CRT screen door effect in the center sections and the color separation effect in the main header.
  • VSCode + LiveServer
    • My text editor of choice. Open-source and cross-platform. Does the job.
    • LiveServer is a VSCode extension that allows you to host a local server and see your changes in real-time. Not essential but saves time.
  • Ezgif
    • Online GIF editor. Lots of useful tools like cropping, flipping, compressing, and converting to/from MP4.
    • Used for Ame-Chan's icon in the music player and the Minecraft skin gif.
  • Fonts
    • TrixieCyrG-Plain: Contrary to popular belief, this is the actual font used in the “Serial Experiments Lain” title card, not LoveLetter TW.
    • LoveLetter TW is still used for the central section titles, though ;P
  • Buttons
    • Those little 88x31 gifs you see all over sites like mine, very nostalgic!
    • Anlucas
    • The 88x31 GIF Collection
    • The Largest 88x31 GIF Collection on the Internet
  • Stamps
    • Gligar
